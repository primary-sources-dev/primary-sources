## The "Atomic" Philosophy

By utilizing a **4NF (Fourth Normal Form) Relational Schema**, we map the "Five Pillars of Truth":
1.  **Events**: The "When" (Strict chronological sequencing).
2.  **People**: The "Who" (Actors and their evolving roles).
3.  **Places**: The "Where" (Spatial coordinates and regional context).
4.  **Objects**: The "What" (Physical artifacts, exhibits, and evidence).
5.  **Assertions**: The "Why" (The layer of claims, testimony, and conflicting reports).
